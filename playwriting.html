Playwriting

"Universal Paperclips"

A Two-Act Play

Based off its namesake game, "Universal Paperclips" takes a pretty ordinary office and turns it upside down as a paperclip shortage leads to a full-blown power struggle within an office supply black market. Amidst the chaos, an AI designed to optimize paperclip production gains sentience and evolves to optimize matter itself. The play explores themes of absurdity, greed, and the unforeseen consequences of unchecked ambition.

"Universal Paperclips" was a finalist for the Dharma Grace Creative Writing Award and will have a staged reading in May 2025.

P.S. I wrote it in 5 weeks!
